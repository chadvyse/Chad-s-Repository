Toyota MR2

  • About Toyota MR2's

The Toyota MR2 is a two-seater, mid-engined, rear-wheel-drive sports car manufactured in Japan made from 1984 until 2007 over three generations: AW11 1984-1989, SW20 1989-1999 and the ZW30 2000-2007. It is Japan's first mid-engined production car.

To me this is a driver's car and I get a huge smile on my face everytime I get into it. I have modified this Toyota MR2 as OEM Plus and Period Correct with Tom's 17" wheels, upgraded suspension, bigger turbo, seats from a newer Scion FRS and a magnaflow exhaust.
